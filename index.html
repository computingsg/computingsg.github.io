History

Computing education winter in Singapore

Teacher education

  • 199x: small (typically 2-3 student teachers) batches of pre-service Computing in NIE (no Computing content, general pedagogy related to JC lecture-tutorial system)
  • Late 199?: pre-service Computing ended in NIE (MOE says no demand)
  • 201x - present: NIE offers 2/3-hour unplugged technologies workshop for Computing teachers
  • 2015: To support the launch of the O-level Computing syllabus, Prof Ben Leong with CPDD Computing education unit started Computing Teachers Content Upgrading Course (CTCUC) to convert interested math or science teachers to teach O-level and A-level Computing. The inaugural batch graduated 20 teachers.
  • 2017: Last batch of CTCUC run by Prof Ben Leong and ESTL
  • 2018: No CTCUC
  • 2019-2020: CTCUC outsourced to polytechnics
  • 2021: No CTCUC. NIE prepares to run CTCUC as there was a Computing teacher seconded to NIE OER
  • 2022: NIE GPL ran CTCUC
  • 2023: NIE does not have capacity to sustain CTCUC as seconded Computing teacher was posted back to school
