Our Pedagogy

Self-directed learning with human-centric support

Student Agency
Empowering students to take ownership of their learning journey, make choices, and develop self-regulation skills essential for lifelong learning.
Peer Learning
Collaborative learning environment where students learn from each other, share knowledge, and develop communication and teamwork skills.
Human-Centric Support
Dedicated mentors and educators provide guidance, emotional support, and personalized assistance during the formative high school years.
AI by Your Side
Intelligent AI companions provide 24/7 learning support, personalized assistance, and adaptive guidance to enhance the educational experience.

Learning Methodology

1
Project-Based Discovery
Students begin with real-world projects that spark curiosity and drive learning. No traditional lectures - knowledge is acquired through necessity and exploration.
2
Peer Collaboration
Learning happens through peer-to-peer interaction, code reviews, and collaborative problem-solving. Students teach and learn from each other.
3
Mentor Guidance
Unlike School 42's purely peer-based approach, we provide dedicated mentors who offer guidance, emotional support, and help navigate challenges.
4
AI-Enhanced Learning
AI companions provide personalized tutoring, instant feedback, adaptive content delivery, and intelligent progress tracking to optimize each student's learning journey.
5
Continuous Assessment
Progress is measured through project completion, peer evaluation, and practical application rather than traditional testing methods.

Support Systems

Personal Mentors
Dedicated mentors provide one-on-one guidance and emotional support
Peer Networks
Structured peer support groups and study circles
Wellness Programs
Mental health support and stress management resources
Academic Scaffolding
Structured learning paths with progressive difficulty
Family Engagement

Career Guidance
Future planning and career pathway exploration
AI Learning Assistant
24/7 AI tutoring, instant feedback, and personalized learning paths
